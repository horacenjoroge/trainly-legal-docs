Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Trainly, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile picture
  • Profile Data: Age, fitness goals, preferences, bio
  • Contact Information: For support and communication purposes

Workout and Fitness Data

  • Exercise Data: Workout types, duration, calories burned, distance
  • Progress Tracking: Personal records, achievements, fitness statistics
  • Location Data: GPS routes during workouts (if enabled)
  • Device Data: Health data from connected fitness devices (with permission)

App Usage Information

  • Usage Analytics: App features used, session duration, preferences
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Social Features: Posts, comments, likes, shared content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core App Functionality: Track workouts, calculate progress, provide personalized insights
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your user account
  • Personalization: Customize your app experience and workout recommendations
  • Social Features: Enable sharing workouts and connecting with other users
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
  • Communication: Send important updates, support responses, and notifications
  • Safety: Monitor for inappropriate content and ensure user safety

3. Data Storage & Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/SSL
  • Secure cloud database storage (MongoDB Atlas)
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication systems

Data Location: Your data is stored securely on cloud servers located in the United States, using industry-standard security practices.

Data Retention: We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Trainly may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality:

  • Cloud Storage: MongoDB Atlas for secure data storage
  • Analytics: App performance and usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Maps: Location services for route tracking (with your permission)
  • Authentication: Secure login and account verification services

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly choose to share workout data or posts
  • Social Features: Information you choose to make public through the app's social features
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality)
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with notice to users)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: View and download your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Export your workout data
  • Opt-out: Control marketing communications and notifications
  • Location Control: Enable or disable location tracking

To exercise these rights, visit the app settings or contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

Trainly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email. Please review this policy periodically for updates.

9. International Users

If you are using Trainly from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

Contact Information
